About
me

I’ve always had a creative drive. Drawing, painting, and creating have been part of my life for as long as I can remember — and in many ways, they’re present in everything I do. Previously, I ran my own business focused on watercolor illustrations and also have an administrative background. Today, I’m pursuing a bachelor’s degree in Graphic Design and Web Development and am now in my final year.

Alongside my studies, I work at a company called Add Insight, where I’m involved in various aspects of visual communication. I create manuals, assessment scales, and written materials, and I also handle updates and maintenance of the company’s WordPress website. Additionally, I’ve built landing pages in Myflow connected to the site and create content for newsletters and campaigns.

During my studies, I’ve discovered how incredibly rewarding and essential UX and UI design are. To deepen my knowledge further, I completed the online course UX & Web Design Master Course: Strategy, Design, Development alongside my studies.

I’m a registered freelancer and gladly take on freelance projects within graphic design, illustration, UX/UI, and web design.
